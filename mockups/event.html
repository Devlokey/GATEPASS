ABOUT

Tathva is the annual National Technical Festival of NIT Calicut — one of India's premier technical extravaganzas. Now in its 26th edition, Tathva '26 brings together the brightest minds from across the country for a day of innovation, competition, and collaboration.

From cutting-edge workshops and paper presentations to robotics battles and hackathons, Tathva offers something for every engineering mind. With over 50+ events spanning technical, cultural, and competitive domains, this is the one fest you can't miss.

Come build, compete, and connect with India's top student community.
